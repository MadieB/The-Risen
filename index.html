The Risen Synopsis

Darkness has spread across the world of Etheria. People have gone missing, war threatens many kingdom, and evil villains plot for the complete destruction of the world. Ancient gods foresee a prophecy that'll bring hope to the people. Six chosen heroes, known as the risen, are said to join together to defeat the darkness. Will these six strangers be able to cooperte together, or will they fall apart before their adventure even begins?
