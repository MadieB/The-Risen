Characters

The Risen

Nexoria Stargift

Nexoria, aka Nex, is the crown princess of the Tiefling Kingdom of Demos. She was raised to take over the kingdom after her father, but she wishes for something more. Nex loves to help others and is fascinated by the prophecy. She hoped she was one of the risen, and was shocked to find out that she was. Despite being of royal blood, she is a powerful sorceress.

Ashe

Ashe is a skilled, tiefling assassin. He was raised by the Shadow Raiders and trained to kill. His last assignment was to kill the crowned Demos princess, however he could not follow through. Shadows ally themselves with the stealth assassin in the mists of battle.
