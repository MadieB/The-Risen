Characters

The Risen

Nexoria Stargift

Nexoria, aka Nex, is the crown princess of the Tiefling Kingdom of Demos. She was raised to take over the kingdom after her father, but she wishes for something more. Nex loves to help others and is fascinated by the prophecy. She hoped she was one of the risen, and was shocked to find out that she was. Despite being of royal blood, she is a powerful sorceress.

Ashe

Ashe is a skilled, tiefling assassin. He was raised by the Shadow Raiders and trained to kill. His last assignment was to kill the crowned Demos princess, however he could not follow through. Shadows ally themselves with the stealth assassin in the mists of battle.

Farieth is a beautiful elven priest. Born and raised in the city of Rivelle, she trained in healing magic with the priests of the Celestial church. She is young compared to most elves but she is still wiser that most of the other risen. Her healing magic is second to no one.

Garreth Romaeve

Garreth is a battle trained warrior. He was raised in the gladiator pits of Kronos. There he honed his skill with a blade and rose through the ranks. After surviving for years, he was released. Now in the world, he uses his skills to protect the innocent people, earning him praise as a hero in the town of Kronos.

Storm

Storm is a tribesman like many other shifters. He was born and raised away from normal society, so he is slow to trust others. When he was a young boy, his village was attacked and his family was killed. He escaped with his life. Storm wandered the forests for months before meeting a stranger. This stranger took in the small child, and raised him in the ways of the wild. Storm was a natural hunter, and was very skilled with a bow.

Pierce Steinman

Pierce was a master at the martial arts. Students from around the world traveled to his dojo to learn from the great master. He had other plans however. Pierce has always had a problem with drinking, and would often be drunk when teaching. Finally after his 200th birthday, he decided to give up teaching. He traveled the world, hoping to find the others of the great dragon's prophecy. Now he uses the time he has left to save the world, while still enjoying his favorite ale.
