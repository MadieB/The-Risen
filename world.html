The World of Etheria

The Prophecy

As darkness falls across the land

The risen shall rise on wings of hope

Let sight not reign upon great wisdom

Nor looks deceive for trust must gain

Pure light shines with grace and hope

Champions rise with soul, not theirs

A heart longs for peace and love, not jewels or gold

A beacon of light dimmed by dark

For battle comes of sun and moon

Tests of trust come deep within

Will light prevail with love alone

or will fate rest in shadowed claws
